Forgot password flow added.

Backend routes:
POST /api/auth/forgot-password
PUT /api/auth/reset-password/:resettoken

Frontend routes:
/forgot-password
/reset-password/:token

If EMAIL_HOST, EMAIL_PORT, EMAIL_USER, and EMAIL_PASS are not configured in backend/.env,
the forgot-password page will show a temporary development reset link after submit.

Optional backend .env values for real email sending:
CLIENT_URL=http://localhost:5173
EMAIL_HOST=smtp.gmail.com
EMAIL_PORT=465
EMAIL_USER=your-email@example.com
EMAIL_PASS=your-app-password
